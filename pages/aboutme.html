About me:


As an artist, I often aim to make work that people can engage with on multiple levels. I've had a love for the idea of modularity as far as I can remember, being able to put things together and reassemble them in any number of ways to make something brand new. I do this in my present work, taking inspiration from various media and artwork that I enjoy and the things that I learn, and building them into something brand new. My current artistic mediums range from physical and digital illustrations to more experimental new media works like interactive digital content in order to both explore how my ideas translate in different mediums and to grow my own artistic skill further. I believe change and growth is a key part of the human experience, and I aim to always consistently learn and grow from my experiences, and reapply those lessons back into work I produce in the future.
